Paid Contracts

We offer small, paid-contracts, often with other partners, especially for individuals, students, or families who experience health and/or social disadvantages.

Specifically, IEDI digital media contracts allow individuals with disabilities to easily put their expertise to use, over the internet, with the freedom of working remotely anywhere in the world.

Digital Media & Art Has No Global Boundary

We specifically invite applicants who experience barriers in traditional, face-to-face or on-site employment. You may wish to self-disclose as much or as little information about your situation, as IEDI prioritizes paid contracts as a supportive tool to accomplish equality in access-to-income. Please note all disclosed information will be verified during interview.
